About Me

A brief overview of my journey in STEM.

My love for STEM started with curiosity for knowledge. My pursuit of environmental sustainability started with birdwatching. My passion for biotechnology began with serving others who have hearing loss like me, and continues to grow.

Thanks to others around me, I have had the chance to devote myself to STEM through various ways: clubs, projects, internships, and service, as well as just for fun in my free time. My aspirations have led me through a half-lifetime-long journey and will continue to lead me in college. I hope my experiences can be inspiring or helpful to others.
